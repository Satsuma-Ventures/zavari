Zavari's signature insight in component form: shows whether a bet ladders to the north star. The mark is the triangulation motif — three signal lines that resolve to a single point when coherent and split apart as conflict grows — so misalignment is intuitive at a glance, calm rather than alarming.

```jsx
<CoherenceFlag state="coherent" detail="Ladders to: Activation north star" />
<CoherenceFlag state="drifting" detail="Weak link to north star" />
<CoherenceFlag state="conflict" label="Conflicts with retention bet" />
```

- `coherent` (cyan, lines converge) · `drifting` (azure) · `conflict` (warning, lines split).
- State is always paired with a word — never color alone.
- Use inline in roadmap rows, brief headers, and coherence views.
