The default grouping surface — white, soft navy shadow, 1px Cool Gray border. Never give a card a colored background; put color in icons and accents inside it.

```jsx
<Card eyebrow="Coherence" title="Pricing bet conflicts with retention goal">
  Two active bets pull in opposite directions. Resolve before the planning lock.
</Card>
<Card title="Connected source" interactive footer={<Button size="sm" variant="ghost">Configure</Button>}>
  Amplitude · syncing every 6h
</Card>
```

- 24px padding, 6px radius. `interactive` adds hover elevation.
- Title is Serif SemiBold Navy; body is Sans Regular Document Black.
